Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for a simple and quick lasagna. It is based on the original italian recipe, making it a delicious and easy meal .

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Fresh parsley and basil leaves
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Lasagna leafs
  • Mozzerella, parmesan

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the leafs
  3. Layer the lasagna
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving
